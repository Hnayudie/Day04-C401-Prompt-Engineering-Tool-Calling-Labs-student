## Source tiers

- Tier 1: primary source, official blog, documentation, paper, dataset, regulatory filing.
- Tier 2: reputable reporting that links to primary evidence.
- Tier 3: social posts, forum posts, anonymous claims, screenshots, and unverified summaries.
- Viral tweets and other viral social posts are Tier 3 evidence.
- Tier 3 can be used as signal only. Do not present it as confirmed fact without Tier 1 or Tier 2 support.

## Citation format

- Every factual item in a digest needs a source URL.
- If evidence is weak, label it as early signal or unverified.
- If a claim comes from a social post, cite the post URL and clearly mark it as a social signal.

## arXiv citation

- arXiv is useful for early research discovery.
- arXiv papers are not automatically peer reviewed.
- When citing arXiv, mention paper title, arXiv ID, source URL, and whether the claim is from the authors.
